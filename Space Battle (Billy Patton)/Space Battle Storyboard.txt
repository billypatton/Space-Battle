Epic Space battle - by Billy Patton C08436789



THE SETUP


there are two sides; The Humans and The Aliens

both sides have 2 ship types; fighter ships and destroyers, both teams have 10 fighters and 2 destroyers




THE FIGHT

- the main battle will commence when the alien race comes in contact with the human race.

- the fighters will swarm/ flock around eachother

- the destroyers move slowly around the outside of the battle

- the destroyers battle like old timey ships

- cinematic camera at start

- once battle starts, switch camera randomly to follow indiviual fighter ships

- if all goes well, fights will be random and a winner will not be predisposed.
